Go Abroad!
Global Citizen Program
AIESEC offers a Global Citizen program as one of our internship programs. This program is an excellent way to experience a new culture. This program spans from 6 weeks to 5 months and offers a great way to immerse oneself into the culture. Due to the relatively short time frame on these internships, it is a great way to spend a summer or a post-graduate semester.

These internships consist of working with the community in the host country. From teaching English to elementary school children in China to running a cultural camp in Russia, these internships are amazing for those who feel like giving back.

Global Talent Program
AIESEC also offers a Talent Program. Meant for those who already have some experience with technology or business these internships are meant to not only provide suitable work experience for those who take it, but also be culturally enriching and relevant. We partner with many large name corporations to ensure that you get the best out of your time with AIESEC.

These internships usually last 6-18 months and are meant for upper-level undergraduates who can afford to take some time off or recent post-graduates. These internships also have more requirements than a GCDP because these companies are also looking for a long term investment. These internships are paid well enough to ensure the participant a comfortable stay in the host country.

Process
  1. Selection: In this stage of the process, the applicant will be required to fill in an online application for review. After reviewing their application, our OGX Team will decide which applicants go on for an interview for further screening. Should they pass this stage, the applicants then have to pass through an Assessment Center in which they will be tested in an active simulation.

  2. Raising: This stage is where all the applicants get partnered up with a manager to ensure they know how to properly navigate our extensive database of offers. In this stage, the applicant will be required to sign up to our database and pay the first fee of $150.

  3. Matching: In this stage, the applicant will be in constant contact with our partners for internship opportunities. They will go through an online interview and discuss details of their potential internship. When both the applicant and our partner company find the details to be acceptable they exchange Acceptance Notes (AN) which is a Binding Contract

  4. Outgoing Preparation Seminar (OPS) This is a 1 Day seminar in which all the applicants matched will be required to attend. This seminar is meant to both prepare the applicants on the experiences they will have abroad, and to introduce the applicants to each other before they go abroad.

  5. Realization: In this stage the applicants will go abroad and have the time of their lives.

  6. Re-Integration: The final stage of the Going Abroad Process is to simply share your experiences with the rest of AIESEC and help us make the experience better for future applicants.

Details and Requirements
  • Length: Our internships last anywhere from 6 weeks to 18 months

  • Selection Process: This would generally take anywhere between 1 and 3 months to completely finish the process.

  • Requirments: Any student or graduate (up to 2 years) is welcome to apply for an AIESEC Internship.
    The type of internship is dependant on the applicant. Each internship may have a specific set of requirments.

  • Airfare: Airfare is not included in our costs. This being said, AIESEC has a partnership with with StudentUniverse for discounted flights!

  • Arrival: In the host country there will be a local chapter of AIESEC willing to assist you in any way.
